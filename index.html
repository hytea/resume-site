Andrew Hyte Bio

Andrew is a Software Engineer known for taking on challenging projects. Recently, he has been leading a remote team of five at GameOn Technologies, managing large-scale projects like architecting and building a Portal used to manage and customize the conversational AI chat experience and ensuring the frontend team met strict timelines and quality benchmarks. His technical expertise spans modern web technologies, including Typescript, React, Angular, and native web components, all backed by a solid foundation in backend integrations and UI/UX design.

Outside of his professional pursuits, Andrew is committed to continuously improving his skills and staying updated on the latest industry trends.
